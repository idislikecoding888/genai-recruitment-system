# Job Description: Senior Full-Stack Developer

## About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Full-Stack Developer to join our innovative team. You will be responsible for building scalable web applications, mentoring junior developers, and contributing to our architectural decisions.

## Requirements:
- **Experience**: 5+ years in professional software development.
- **Frontend**: Expert knowledge of React.js, TypeScript, and modern CSS (Tailwind/Framer Motion).
- **Backend**: Proficiency in Node.js and Python (FastAPI/Django).
- **Databases**: Experience with PostgreSQL and MongoDB.
- **AI/ML (Bonus)**: Familiarity with LangChain, OpenAI API, or building RAG systems.
- **Soft Skills**: Strong problem-solving skills, excellent communication, and a team-player attitude.

## Responsibilities:
- Design and implement robust, reusable components.
- Optimize application for maximum speed and scalability.
- Collaborate with other team members and stakeholders.
